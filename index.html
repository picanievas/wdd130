Pica Nievas | WDD 130

Hello! My name is Pica Nievas and I am from Austin Texas. I enjoy playing board games, making cakes and being wtih my family. Some of the board games that I like to play are on the longer side usually more than two hours long. My favorite board game right now is probably Wonderland Wars, the theme of the game being Alice in Wonderland. Playing board games is also a great time to gather together and spend time with my family which is another thing that I love to do. I also love to make cakes. I have run my own cake decorating business from my home for 8 years. My favorite types of cakes to make are wedding cakes. I love how big and dramatic they are. Also I love to design with the fresh flowers that many cakes use so that is always an added fun for me.

Texas

Texas is one of the largest states in the US. Austin is the capitol and is also known as the live music capitol. Some people might think of Texas as a dusty desert landscape and while those areas are part of Texas, Austin is an oasis of green trees, rivers and lakes.
